Connie Springer

Survey Corps • Loyal and Quick-Witted

  • Affiliation: Survey Corps
  • Origin: Ragako Village
  • Traits: Agility, humor, loyalty

Description

Connie Springer is a member of the Survey Corps known for his boundless energy and quick reflexes. Though often seen as lighthearted, his loyalty and bravery in the face of tragedy mark his deep commitment to his comrades and humanity’s survival.

Achievements

Survived numerous expeditions outside the walls, aided in critical missions against Titans, and displayed unwavering courage through personal loss.

Strengths

  • High mobility and reflexes.
  • Strong team spirit.
  • Adaptable in combat.
