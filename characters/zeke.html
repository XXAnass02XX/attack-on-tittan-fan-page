Zeke Yeager

“Beast Titan.” Warchief of the Marleyan Warriors.

  • Affiliation: Marleyan Warriors
  • Alias: Beast Titan, Warchief
  • Clan: Yeager (royal blood via Dina Fritz)

Description

Zeke Yeager is a strategic prodigy and the inheritor of the Beast Titan. Calm, calculating, and ruthlessly pragmatic, he orchestrates campaigns with chilling precision. Zeke’s royal lineage grants him the power to command Pure Titans with a scream when his spinal fluid is present—an ability he leverages in high-stakes gambits. Beneath the composed exterior lies a conflicted idealist whose vision for ending suffering drives his most controversial choices.

Achievements

As Marley’s Warchief, Zeke led the Warriors to several major victories and refined Titan warfare. His long-range “stone bombardment” made the Beast Titan a terrifying artillery platform. He infiltrated Paradis operations and enabled mass Titan transformations via tainted wine, repeatedly turning the tide. His coordination with Eren Yeager nearly reshaped the world order.

Family

Son of Grisha Yeager and Dina Fritz, Zeke is the half-brother of Eren Yeager. Through Dina, he carries the blood of the Fritz royal line, unlocking unique Beast Titan abilities. His complicated upbringing and betrayal of his parents’ movement forged his utilitarian worldview.

Strengths

  • Artillery Mastery: Devastating long-range throwing accuracy and power.
  • Royal-Blood Ability: Titan control via spinal-fluid activation and a scream.
  • Grand Strategy: Patient planning, misinformation, and multi-phase operations.
  • Composure: Cold reading of opponents and decisive risk management.

Weaknesses

  • Overconfidence: Underestimates elite opponents in close quarters.
  • Specialization: Reliance on ranged combat and scream conditions.
  • Ideological Rigidity: Ends-justify-the-means reasoning blindsides alternatives.
