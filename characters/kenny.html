Kenny Ackerman

“Kenny the Ripper.” Leader of the Anti-Personnel Control Squad.

  • Affiliation: Military Police — 1st Interior Squad
  • Alias: Kenny the Ripper
  • Clan: Ackerman

Description

Kenny Ackerman is a legendary urban-combat specialist whose name spread in whispers through the Underground. Charismatic and ruthless, he modernized human-vs-human conflict inside the Walls, pioneering anti-personnel ODM tactics. Though he trained Levi in brutal pragmatism, Kenny chased power for his own reasons, masking a complicated heart beneath swagger and vice.

Achievements

Kenny organized and led the Interior Squad’s Anti-Personnel unit, introducing rapid-fire sidearms and specialized ODM gear that transformed close-quarters tactics. He executed covert operations for the monarchy, matched the Scouts in vertical city battles, and left an indelible mark on military doctrine within the Walls.

Family

An Ackerman by blood and uncle to Levi, Kenny raised him in the Underground before parting ways. His sister, Kuchel Ackerman, was Levi’s mother. Like other Ackermans, Kenny exhibited the clan’s combat awakening—preternatural battlefield instincts when driven to act.

Strengths

  • Urban Warfare: Mastery of anti-personnel ODM and gunplay in tight spaces.
  • Tactical Improvisation: Ambushes, feints, and ruthless pursuit tactics.
  • Ackerman Physiology: Surging focus and reflexes under lethal pressure.
  • Leadership: Trains and coordinates squads for vertical-city engagements.

Weaknesses

  • Obsessed with Power: Personal ambitions distort long-term judgment.
  • Human Limits: Vulnerable outside ideal terrain or when wounded.
  • Estrangement: Connections fray under his self-serving choices.
