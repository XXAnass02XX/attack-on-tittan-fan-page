Levi Ackerman

“Humanity’s Strongest Soldier.” Captain of the Survey Corps.

  • Affiliation: Survey Corps
  • Alias: Captain Levi
  • Clan: Ackerman

Description

Levi Ackerman is among the most formidable figures in Attack on Titan, widely known as “Humanity’s Strongest Soldier.” A Survey Corps Captain, he’s exceptionally skilled with Omni-Directional Mobility (ODM) gear. Stoic and blunt—paired with a legendary obsession for cleanliness—Levi nonetheless carries deep compassion and a fierce sense of duty shaped by a harsh upbringing in the Underground. His loyalty, tactical clarity, and complex humanity make him a central, enduring presence in the series.

Achievements

A pillar of the Survey Corps, Levi led decisive victories against Titan Shifters—most famously subduing the Beast Titan (Zeke Yeager) multiple times, honoring his vow to Commander Erwin Smith. His leadership and unmatched combat prowess preserved countless soldiers and helped navigate the Corps through political turmoil and the final, climactic battles.

Family

A descendant of the Ackerman clan—persecuted for their resistance to the Founding Titan—Levi was raised in the Underground by his uncle, Kenny Ackerman, who trained him in close-quarters combat before abandoning him. His mother, Kuchel Ackerman, died when he was young. Through this lineage he is distantly related to Mikasa Ackerman, sharing the same surge-like battle instinct.

Strengths

  • Unrivaled Combat Skill: Mastery of ODM gear and a unique, rapid blade style.
  • Ackerman Awakening: Instinctive “do what must be done” battle clarity.
  • Mental Fortitude: Calm, ruthless focus amid catastrophic loss.
  • Duty to Comrades: Ensures sacrifices are never in vain.

Weaknesses

  • Relentless Self-Expectations: Shoulders immense responsibility and guilt.
  • Injury Risk: Hyper-aggressive maneuvers can exact a physical toll.
