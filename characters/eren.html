Eren Yeager

Attack Titan • Founding Titan • “If you win, you live.”

  • Affiliation: Survey Corps (former)
  • Aliases: Attack Titan, Founding Titan
  • Drive: Freedom at any cost

Description

Eren’s journey runs from vengeance-driven recruit to world-altering Titan shifter. Gifted with both the Attack and Founding Titans, he challenges fate, history, and the world’s borders in pursuit of freedom, forcing friends and foes alike to confront impossible choices.

Achievements

Unlocked the paths of the Founding Titan, coordinated large-scale operations, and changed the global balance through decisive—often controversial—actions that redefined the conflict.

Strengths

  • Indomitable will and resilience.
  • Power of two Titans and mastery over paths.
  • Strategic boldness under pressure.
